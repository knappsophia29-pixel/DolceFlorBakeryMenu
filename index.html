About Me

About Sophia

Hello! I'm Sophia Knapp, the founder and baker behind Dolce Flor Bakery. I have a passion for baking nutritious, feel-good desserts that are truly delicious.

I'm currently a freshman at Horace Greeley High School where I play varsity tennis. Tennis is another passion of mine that keeps me active and energized. I love baking treats for my tennis team! I also love to play the piano, do math, and spend time with my family.

My desserts are not only for those who follow a gluten-free diet, but for anyone who wants to eat desserts that taste amazing and make them feel amazing. I make each dessert myself in my home kitchen, where I am extremely careful to avoid cross-contamination. I hope you enjoy the desserts I bake just as much as I enjoy making them. Thank you so much for ordering!

by Sophia Knapp
